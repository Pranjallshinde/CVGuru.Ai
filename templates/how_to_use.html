How to Use CVGuru.AI

Master your interview preparation in 3 simple steps

1

Upload Your Resume & Select Job Role

  • Upload your resume in PDF format
  • Select your target job role from dropdown
  • Ensure your resume is clear and readable
  • Include relevant skills and experience

Get Personalized Questions

  • AI analyzes your resume content
  • Generates 15 relevant interview questions

• Describe your recent project

3

Get Sample Answers

  • Click "Generate Sample Answers" button
  • AI creates personalized answers using STAR method
  • Practice with the provided answers
  • Customize answers to your experience
STAR Method:
Situation - Context
Task - What needed to be done
Action - What you did
Result - The outcome
